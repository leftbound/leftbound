Gear Talk. Going outdoors really isn’t, or at least shouldn’t be, about gear, but unless you go butt naked in the woods, relying on your hopefully uncompromized and honed survival skills, some essentials are still needed. And of course you cannot go cycling without a bike, paddling without a raft or ski touring without skis. And so on. Hence the gear talk.

I’m a huge fan of cottage industry and handicraft, and anything built locally, where ever that might be, by small businesses. And regardless of the company size or origin, original designs, game changing products and smart use of resources and material also gets my attention.

A thorough real-world focused field tester with keen eye on design, materials, usability and durability. Your company’s product would be in good and capable hands, should you be interested in offering something topical for a test and review - Contact me for details.
